Abdul Sattar Edhi

1928 - 2016

Angel of Mercy

Abdul Sattar Edhi was a legendary Pakistani humanitarian, philanthropist, and social activist. Born in 1928, he dedicated his life to alleviating suffering and helping those in He founded the Edhi Foundation, which has become the largest social welfare organization in Pakistan. The foundation operates a vast network of ambulance services, homeless shelters, orphanages, and rehabilitation centers, providing vital services to countless people. Edhi is particularly recognized for his work during natural disasters and emergencies, mobilizing resources to offer relief and support. He is widely revered in Pakistan as a national hero and is considered one of the world's greatest humanitarians for his tireless efforts to serve those most vulnerable.

Global Recognition and Legacy:

  • Edhi's unwavering commitment to humanitarianism earned him international recognition. He received numerous awards, including the prestigious Ramon Magsaysay Award, often referred to as the "Nobel Prize of Asia."
